10+ Years Building Infrastructure That Never Fails.

I'm Annas, a Senior IT Infrastructure & Security Engineer Profesional with over a decade of hands-on experience across Network, Server, Cloud, and Security domains — based in Jakarta, Indonesia, serving the coal mining and energy industry.
